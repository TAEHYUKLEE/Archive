  Description: As a graduation project, our team set out to create a percussion instrument that embodies rhythm and is easily accessible to beginners. As a result of our work, we were able to successfully complete the project with a great final presentation and even secured a purchase offer. My involvement in this project was to handle the portion of the prototype that controls the light to rhythm, as well as the portion that adds percussion sounds to the end product. above all, the design team, public relations team, software team, and hardware team collaborated closely with the team members in this project, which reminded me of the importance of collaboration.
